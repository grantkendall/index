I like to think and I like to think I think about the web differently. From my perspective, most of the web looks pretty similar. How is it possible a tool that everyone on Earth uses looks the same? How is it possible that an edgeless, infinitely expansive medium settled for the same? That doesn't seem right to me. What does it mean to scroll? What does a hyperlink mean in the age of data extraction? I like to think making art that questions itself makes my opinion different than other designers. I could be wrong about that; wouldn't it be fun to find out?


I'm interested in the history of the web. I'm interested in the history of communication networks. I feel an obligation to ensure my participation in web-building is constructive whilst advocating for best carbon practices.
